## **What is the Shoppable video section?** 

The "Shoppable video" section is an interactive and engaging feature designed to enhance the shopping experience. This section allows you to play and pause a video, with selected products sliding in from left to right based on specific timestamps you set in the video. It also offers a quick buy option and a rewind button for desktop users, making it easy for customers to purchase featured products directly from the video.

## Main settings

 **General** 

* **Heading:** Enter the heading for the shoppable video section.
* **Make section full width:** Enable this setting to make the video span the entire width of the container.
* **Enable quick add button:** Add a quick buy option for products displayed in the video.
* **Enable rewind button on desktop:** Add a rewind button for desktop users.
* **Hide brand name:** Check this option to hide the brand name in the section.

 **Shopify-Hosted Video** 

* **Shopify-hosted video:** Upload or select a video to play in the background.

 **Section Height** 

* **Desktop fixed height:** Adjust the fixed height for desktop view.
* **Mobile fixed height:** Adjust the fixed height for mobile view.
* **Enable full screen mode:** Enable this option to allow the video to play in full screen.

 **Colors** 

* **Heading color:** Choose the color for the heading text.
* **Background color:** Set the background color for the section.
* **Background opacity:** Adjust the opacity of the background color.
* **Text color:** Choose the color for the text.

 **Section Spacing** 

* **Distance from the top:** Adjust the spacing from the top of the section.
* **Distance from the bottom:** Adjust the spacing from the bottom of the section.
