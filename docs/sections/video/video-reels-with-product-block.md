## **Video Reels with product block**

<DocVideo src="/img/docs/876896f90702.webm" autoPlay playsInline muted loop style={{width: "100%", marginBottom: "20px", borderRadius: "8px"}} />

The **Video Reels with Product** block brings a social-media-style experience directly to your product pages.
It allows you to showcase multiple short videos in a **reel format** , similar to TikTok or Instagram Reels  -  customers can watch full-screen videos, scroll through them, and even add featured products to the cart directly from the video.

Built as a **native feature** , not an external app, this block ensures smooth performance and quick loading times.
It's a modern, high-impact way to engage shoppers, boost conversion rates, and make your product pages feel alive and interactive.

## How to set up Video Reels with product block?

* Add a Video Reels block to the **Product Information** section
* Select the **Shopify-hosted videos** you'd like to display.

<DocVideo src="/img/docs/9049d383d0be.webm" controls playsInline muted loop style={{width: "100%", marginBottom: "20px", borderRadius: "8px"}} />

* (Optional) Add **featured products** to accompany each video

<DocVideo src="/img/docs/bbba644bed4b.webm" controls playsInline muted loop style={{width: "100%", marginBottom: "20px", borderRadius: "8px"}} />

* Customize the section appearance using the additional settings. You can:
* Show or hide **Play/Pause** button.
* Show or hide **Mute/Unmute** button
* Enable **Auto advance on video end** – when a video ends, it smoothly scrolls to the next one.
* Add a **Quick Buy** button for products.
* Hide the **Product vendor**
