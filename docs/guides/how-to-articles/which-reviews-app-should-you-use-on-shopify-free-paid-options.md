## Which Reviews App Should You Use on Shopify? (Free & Paid Options)

Want to boost trust and increase sales? Adding star-rated product reviews to your Shopify store is a must. But which app should you choose - free or paid?

### **Top Free Review Apps** 

#### **1. Judge.me (Free plan available)** 

* Star ratings, photo reviews, SEO snippets
* Automated email review requests
* Highly customizable widgets
* Best for: Stores of all sizes looking for great value without paying

#### **2. Loox (Free plan for up to 30 orders/month)** 

* Photo reviews with a sleek, visual layout
* Review request emails
* Best for: Small stores focused on visual content

#### **3. Yotpo (Free plan available)** 

* Basic review collection and display
* Integration with other Yotpo tools (loyalty, SMS)
* Best for: Brands planning to scale with advanced marketing tools

#### **Paid Options for More Features** 

* **Judge.me Awesome Plan – $15/month** : More customization, video reviews, Google Shopping support
* **Loox – from $9.99/month** : Video reviews, more review requests, upsells
* **Yotpo Growth Plans** : Unlocks social push, Q&A, Google Ads reviews
