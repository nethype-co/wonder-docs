## **Sharing a Preview of Your Shopify Theme** 

Before you decide to make your new theme live, it's often a good idea to get feedback from others or seek assistance from theme developers. Shopify allows you to create a preview link, giving you the ability to share access to an unpublished theme and gather valuable feedback in the right direction.

### **Here's How to Share a Shopify Theme Preview:** 

1. **Access Your Shopify Admin:** 
- Log in to your Shopify admin panel.
2. **Navigate to Themes:** 
- In your Shopify admin, click on the "Themes" option.
3. **Choose the Theme to Share:** 
- Scroll down to the "More Themes" section, where you'll see a list of your themes. Choose the theme you want to share.
4. **Preview the Theme:** 
- After selecting the theme, click on the "..." (ellipsis) button to access the theme options.
5. **Access the Preview Bar:** 
- At the bottom of the preview page, you'll find a preview bar. This bar contains three buttons, one of which is labeled "Share preview."
6. **Generate the Share Link:** 
- Click the "Share preview" button, and a window will appear containing the share link for this theme.
7. **Copy and Share:** 
- To share the preview link, simply click the "Copy link" button. You can then share this link with anyone you want, whether it's for feedback or assistance.
