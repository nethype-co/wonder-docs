## **Price block** 

The "Price" block on your Shopify store can be customized to show discounted prices alongside the original, with the savings percentage clearly highlighted. A note can be added to assure customers that taxes are included in the final price, ensuring transparency. Text size is adjustable for optimal clarity on any device.
