## **Rating block** 

The "Customize rating block" is a feature designed for Shopify store owners who want to leverage customer feedback effectively. By integrating a product rating system, you can encourage buyers to leave reviews, fostering trust and transparency for your brand. This block allows you to display ratings prominently on each product page, which can influence purchasing decisions and provide valuable insights into your customers' experiences. Installation is straightforward: simply add a compatible rating app from the Shopify app store to activate this feature and start showcasing customer ratings directly on your site.
