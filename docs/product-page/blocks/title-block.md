## **Title block**

Maximize the impact of your product page with our customizable title block feature. Tailor your title to your brand's voice by adding your vendor's name to boost recognition. Adjust the title text size with precision for both large and mobile screens, ensuring your message is clear and effective no matter the device. With this tool, you can effortlessly refine your product titles to capture your audience's attention and enhance your online presence.
