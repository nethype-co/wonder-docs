# v1.6.0 — January 23, 2025

## Added

- **Wellness Preset**: a beautifully curated preset designed to inspire calm and focus, ideal for health and wellness brands
- **Testimonial Video Reels** section for showcasing customer feedback with engaging video testimonials
- **Products Slider** section with two connected sliders for product showcases
- **Scrolling Text Banner** section: add eye-catching scrolling text for announcements, slogans, or promotional messages
- **Product Variations Block**: display product variations with icons on product pages
- **Feature Tags Block**: highlight unique product features and benefits
- **Sticky Header Blur Options**: added blur effects for the sticky header to enhance usability and navigation aesthetics
- Video embedding capability within the **Product Benefits** section

## Fixes and Improvements

- Enhanced layout and functionality for the Features Banner section
- Corrected broken links in second-level Mega Menu items
- Resolved styling issues with the gift card logo
